Universal Studios Japan

Universal Studio Japan is located in Osaka and is one of the largest theme parks in japan. Similar to the US parks, there are multiple sections each themed after different IPs. My favorite was the Harry Potter section and Super Nintendo World.

Tokyo SkyTree

Tokyo SkyTree in Tokyo is the tallest structure in the metropolitan, standing at 634 meters tall. It overlooks the entirety of Tokyo and you can see up to 43 miles from its viewing platform.

Other sights to see in Osaka includes the Historic Osaka Castle.
The food in Japan is reasonably priced yet deliciously filling.
